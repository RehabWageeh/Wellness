Slide 01

Find an exercise routine that you enjoy: This can be anything from running, swimming, dancing, yoga, or weight lifting.
By finding an activity that you enjoy, you will be more likely to stick to it.

Slide 02

Schedule exercise into your day: Make exercise a part of your daily routine, just like you would with brushing your teeth or taking a shower. This will help you prioritize physical activity and make it a habit.

Slide 03

Set achievable goals: Start small and gradually increase your activity level. For example, start with a 10-minute walk and gradually increase the time or distance.

Slide 04

Make it a social activity: Join a fitness class, sports team or exercise with friends. Having someone to exercise with can be a great motivator.

Slide 05

Mix it up: Variety is key to preventing boredom and staying motivated. Try different types of exercises to challenge your body and keep things interesting.

Slide 06

Take breaks from sitting: Sitting for long periods of time can be harmful to your health. Try to take breaks throughout the day to stand, stretch, and move around.
