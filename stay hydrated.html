Slide 01

Drink plenty of water throughout the day. Aim for at least 8 cups (64 ounces) of water per day.

Slide 02

Include hydrating foods in your diet, such as fruits and vegetables that have a high water content.

Slide 03

Limit your intake of caffeine and alcohol, as they can be dehydrating.

Slide 04

Consider carrying a refillable water bottle with you wherever you go.

Slide 05

Drink water before, during, and after exercise.

Slide 06

Pay attention to your body's thirst cues and drink water when you feel thirsty.

Slide 07

If you are sick, drink plenty of fluids to help your body fight off infection.
