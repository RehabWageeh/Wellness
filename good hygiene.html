Slide 01

Wash your hands regularly: Use soap and water to wash your hands often, especially after using the bathroom, before eating, and after blowing your nose, coughing or sneezing. Scrub your hands for at least 20 seconds and dry them completely.

Slide 02

Cover your mouth and nose when you cough or sneeze: Use a tissue or your elbow to cover your mouth and nose when you cough or sneeze to prevent the spread of germs.

Slide 03

Keep your surroundings clean: Regularly clean and disinfect frequently touched objects and surfaces, such as door handles, phones, keyboards, and countertops.

Slide 04

Practice good oral hygiene: Brush your teeth twice a day and floss daily to prevent dental problems and bad breath.

Slide 05

Take care of your skin: Keep your skin clean and dry to prevent skin infections. Use water to wash your skin and apply moisturizer regularly to keep it hydrated.

Slide 06

Avoid sharing personal items: Don't share personal items, such as towels, razors, or toothbrushes, as they can spread germs and infections.

Slide 07

Stay home when you're sick: If you're sick, stay home to prevent the spread of germs and infections to others.
