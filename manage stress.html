Slide 01

Practice relaxation techniques: Try deep breathing, meditation, yoga, or tai chi to help you relax.

Slide 02

Identify the source of your stress: Make a list of the things that are causing you stress, and try to figure out which ones you can control and which ones you can't.

Slide 03

Talk to someone: Sometimes talking to a friend or family member can help you feel less stressed. If you feel like you need more support, consider talking to a therapist or counselor.

Slide 04

Manage your time: Make a schedule or to-do list to help you manage your time more effectively, and prioritize the tasks that are most important.

Slide 05

Exercise regularly: Exercise can help reduce stress by releasing endorphins, which are natural mood-boosters.

Slide 06

Practice mindfulness: Try to be present in the moment and focus on your senses. This can help you feel more calm and centered.

Slide 07

Avoid unhealthy coping mechanisms: Avoid using drugs, alcohol, or other unhealthy coping mechanisms to deal with stress. Instead, find healthy ways to manage your stress, such as exercise, relaxation techniques, or talking to someone.
