Slide 01

Cook at home: Cooking at home allows you to control the ingredients in your meals and can help you make healthier choices.

Slide 02

Eat a variety of foods from all the food groups: To ensure that you're getting all the nutrients that your body needs, eat a balanced mix of foods from each food group, including fruits, vegetables, whole grains, lean proteins, and healthy fats.

Slide 03

Pay attention to portion sizes: Eating too much of even healthy foods can lead to weight gain. Use measuring cups and spoons or a food scale to measure your portions, or learn to estimate portion sizes.

Slide 04

Eat plenty of fruits and vegetables: These foods are high in vitamins, minerals, and fiber, and low in calories. They can help you feel full and prevent overeating.

Slide 05

Choose whole grains: Whole grains are a good source of fiber and other nutrients, and can help you feel full for longer. Choose whole grain breads, pasta, and cereals.

Slide 06

Limit processed and sugary foods: These foods can be high in calories and low in nutrients. Limit your intake of processed foods, sugary drinks, and sweets.

Slide 07

Stay hydrated: Drinking plenty of water is important for overall health and can help you feel full and prevent overeating.
