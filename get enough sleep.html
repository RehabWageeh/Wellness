Slide 01

Stick to a sleep schedule: Go to bed and wake up at the same time every day, even on weekends.

Slide 02

Create a relaxing bedtime routine: This can include taking a warm bath, reading a book, or listening to calming music.

Slide 03

Make your bedroom comfortable: Keep your bedroom cool, dark, and quiet. Invest in a comfortable mattress and pillows.

Slide 04

Limit caffeine and alcohol: Avoid consuming caffeine and alcohol in the evening, as they can disrupt your sleep.

Slide 05

Exercise regularly: Exercise can help improve the quality of your sleep, but avoid exercising too close to bedtime.

Slide 06

Avoid screens before bedtime: The blue light emitted from electronic devices can disrupt your sleep. Turn off your devices at least an hour before bedtime.

Slide 07

Manage stress: Practice relaxation techniques, such as meditation or deep breathing, to help manage stress and promote better sleep.
