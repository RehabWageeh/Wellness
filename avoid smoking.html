Slide 01

Find healthy alternatives: Replace smoking or drinking with healthier alternatives, such as chewing gum, drinking water, or engaging in physical activity.

Slide 02

Avoid triggers: Identify triggers that may make you want to smoke or drink, such as social situations or stress, and find ways to avoid or manage them.

Slide 03

Seek support: Surround yourself with people who support your decision to quit smoking or drinking, and consider joining a support group or seeking professional help.

Slide 04

Set goals: Set specific goals for quitting smoking or drinking, and track your progress. Celebrate your achievements along the way.

Slide 05

Stay positive: Focus on the benefits of quitting smoking or drinking, such as better health and saving money, and stay positive even if you experience setbacks. Remember that quitting is a process, and every day without smoking or drinking is a step in the right direction.
